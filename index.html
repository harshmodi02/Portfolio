Mega Project - Entrepreneurship

AlumNextDoor

The goal of the platform is to connect graduate school applicants to students and alumni (who we call mentors) from their dream school who have similar background/goals to get help from them for their graduate school applications.

The idea of AlumNextDoor was born from the belief that the current consultancy services do not provide as much value for money and that a person who has already trodden the path to get into their dream school could help out an applicant much better at a fraction of the cost / FREE.

We are a young team trying to disrupt the consultancy market! I, as the techy Co-Founder, handle the entire technical aspect of the platform which majorly involves building the platform and keeping it up and running. I am also involved in the Marketing and Sales for the platform.
